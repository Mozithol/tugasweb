Conquest

Large-scale warfare with multiple capture points. Team-based combat to secure objectives and reduce the enemy's tickets.

Breakthrough

Attack and defend dynamic sectors in this intense mode. The attacking team aims to capture all sectors, while defenders try to hold them off.

Hazard Zone

A tense squad-based mode focused on high-stakes objectives and valuable rewards. Be cautious, as the map is filled with AI threats and other squads.
